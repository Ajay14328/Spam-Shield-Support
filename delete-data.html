Data Deletion Instructions for Spam Shield

At Spam Shield, we respect your privacy and give you control over your data. If you have an account with us or have provided any personal information, you have the right to request the deletion of your data.

How to Request Data Deletion

Step 1: Send an email to [Your Contact Email] from the email address associated with your account.

Step 2: Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".

Step 3: In the body of the email, state clearly that you wish all your personal data associated with your account to be deleted.

What Happens Next?

Once we receive your request:

  • We will process your request within 7 to 14 business days.
  • Your account and any associated personal data stored on our servers will be permanently deleted.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

Important Note on Local Data

Since Spam Shield processes SMS/message data locally on your device for spam detection, uninstalling the app will automatically remove all local app data from your phone. We do not have access to data stored locally on your device.

If you have any questions or issues with this process, please reach out to our support team.
